What Services Do We Provide?

  • Courses
  • Entrepreneurship Services
  • Co-Working Space

We offer a wide range of courses to equip young adults and recent university graduates with the skills necessary to secure employment or establish their own start-up ventures.

  • Basic Computer Skills (Windows OS)
  • Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Powerpoint)
  • Graphic Design
  • AutoCAD
  • Autodesk Revit
  • Coding and Robotics for Children
  • Introduction to Programming in C++
  • Introduction to Programming in Python

Our Entrepreneurship services aims to aid small businesses in their growth by providing a range of services including training, workshops, and seminars. Additionally, we offer research and advisory services to assist small businesses in making informed decisions. Our workshops also promote entrepreneurship as a viable career option for students. Some of the workshops provided in this section include:

  • Start Your Business
  • Get to Know the Business and Institutions
  • The basic elementary of any project
  • Project as a system
  • The successful project
  • Getting to know customer
  • Defining the problem
  • Design thinking
  • Finance statement
  • Marketing
  • Go to market strategy

The Co-working space section aims to assist youth and entrepreneurs in their business ventures by providing high-quality workspaces, trainings, and workshops, allowing them to concentrate, communication, and collaborate effectively, thereby improving their performance. Additionally, this section offers reasonably priced, fully serviced workspaces to the market, with the goal of jumpstarting new businesses.
